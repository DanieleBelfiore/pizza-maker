## **ğŸŒŸ Features**

The application does more than just show you a recipe; it's a fully functional dough calculator:

* **Dynamic Calculator:** Enter the number of pizzas, the desired weight for each dough ball, and the hydration percentage to get the exact amounts of flour, water, yeast, and salt needed.  
* **Step-by-Step Recipe:** A detailed guide for a long-fermentation dough (about 24 hours), from the initial mixing to portioning and stretching the dough.
